About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed roguelike survival game where your objective is simple: stay alive as long as possible against relentless waves of monsters. As you progress, you'll unlock powerful weapons, abilities, and upgrades to help you survive the increasingly difficult challenges.

Inspired by popular roguelike games, Monster Survivors combines fast-paced action with strategic decision-making. Each playthrough offers a unique experience with procedurally generated enemy patterns and random power-ups, ensuring that no two games are ever the same.

Tips & Strategies

Beginner Tips

  • Focus on movement and dodging first - survival is your primary goal
  • Choose complementary weapons that cover different attack patterns
  • Collect all experience gems to level up faster
  • Health pickups are rare - prioritize them when your health is low

Advanced Strategies

  • Learn each enemy's attack patterns and behaviors
  • Create synergies between your weapons and passive items
  • Sometimes it's better to kite enemies into groups before attacking
  • Save area-of-effect abilities for when you're surrounded
